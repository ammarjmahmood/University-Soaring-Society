University Soaring Society

The USS is a student group at Toronto Metropolitan University which allows aviators dreams to take flight. With a comprehensive program involving simulator training, radio licensing, and instruction, the USS allows students to achieve their Government issued glider pilot license at a reduced cost. 

 

 

Toronto Soaring Club

Toronto Soaring  Club is a long-established soaring club west of Orangeville with a proud history. The USS is grateful to have the support of Toronto Soaring Club in developing the next generation of glider pilots..

Youth Flight Canada

For more than 20 years, YFC has focused on granting scholarships to offset the high cost of entering aviation. Their work focuses on providing scholarships to dedicated students pursuing their education, while achieving aviation licensing.
