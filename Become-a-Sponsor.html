Become a Sponsor

We encourage you to donate to the Youth Flight Education Fund through CanadaHelps. Donations made through CanadaHelps directly benefit student pilots at the USS, while following all CRA guidelines. Please click the link below to donate to the USS through Youth Flight Canada.  https://www.canadahelps.org/en/charities/youth-flight-canada-education-fund/
Alternatively, if you have other inquiries about sponsoring the USS, please send an email to uss@mues.ca. We would be ecstatic to have you help student pilots reach new height in their soaring endeavors.
